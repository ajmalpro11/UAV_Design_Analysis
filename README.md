## Overview

This repository documents my virtual internship at **Skyline Aerospace, Coimbatore**, under their **Drone and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (DUAV) Program**. The internship provided hands-on experience across the complete lifecycle of UAV development — from understanding flight fundamentals to designing, simulating, and analyzing real drone assemblies.

🌐 [Skyline Aerospace – DUAV Program](https://skyline-space.com/duav/)

---

## Internship Assignments

### 📘 1. UAV Types & Specifications
- Study of various UAV classifications — Fixed Wing, Rotary Wing, VTOL, Hybrid
- Understanding key specifications: payload, endurance, range, propulsion systems
- Comparison of Micro, Mini, and Industrial UAVs

---

### ✏️ 2. Forces & Moments – Calculations
- Calculation of aerodynamic forces: Lift, Drag, Thrust, and Weight
- Moment analysis and stability assessment
- Application of fundamental flight dynamics equations

---

### 🖊️ 3. SolidWorks – Sketching & Modelling Exercises
- 2D sketching exercises and constraints
- 3D part modelling of drone components
- Feature-based modelling: extrude, revolve, loft, sweep

---

### 🌊 4. Aerofoil Selection – NACA Profiles
- Study of NACA aerofoil series and selection criteria
- Lift and drag coefficient analysis
- Aerofoil geometry generation and import into SolidWorks

---

### 💨 5. CFD Analysis – ANSYS Fluent
- CFD simulation of selected NACA aerofoil
- Boundary condition setup: velocity inlet, pressure outlet
- Visualization of pressure distribution, velocity contours, and lift/drag results

---

### 🔧 6. Drone Assembly – SolidWorks
- Full assembly of drone components in SolidWorks
- Mate constraints and assembly hierarchy
- Exploded view and bill of materials generation

---

### 🧱 7. Structural Analysis – ANSYS Workbench
- Static structural analysis of drone frame
- Mesh generation and boundary condition setup
- Results: Von Mises stress, deformation, and factor of safety
